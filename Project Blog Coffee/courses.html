Coffee Extraction Techniques

Price: Free

Slots: 20

Coffee extraction is an intricate process that involves unlocking the desirable soluble compounds from coffee grounds and infusing them into water, resulting in a delicious and well-balanced cup of coffee.

Workshop for Roasting and Grinding Beans

Price: Free

Slots: 20

The "Workshop for Roasting and Grinding Beans" is an immersive experience that delves into the art and science of transforming raw coffee beans into the aromatic, flavorful beverage we all love. In this hands-on workshop, participants will be introduced to the fascinating journey of coffee from its initial green state to the perfectly roasted beans ready for brewing. During the workshop, attendees will have the opportunity to explore various coffee bean origins, learning about the unique characteristics that define each region's coffee. Expert roasters will guide participants through the roasting process, explaining how temperature, time, and other factors influence the final flavor profile of the coffee. As the freshly roasted beans release their enticing aromas, the workshop will transition into the art of grinding. Participants will discover how the grind size impacts different brewing methods, from pour-over to espresso, and how to achieve the ideal consistency for a truly exceptional cup.

Classic Cappuccino

Price: Free

Slots: 20

The cappuccino is a beloved espresso-based coffee drink that combines rich, bold espresso with velvety steamed milk and a delicate layer of milk foam on top. To create a perfect cappuccino, it's essential to master the art of balancing these three key components. Mastering the cappuccino-making process takes practice and attention to detail. By understanding the theory behind creating this classic coffee beverage, you can experiment and refine the technique to suit your taste preferences. With time, you'll become proficient in crafting a delightful cappuccino that delights your senses with every sip.
